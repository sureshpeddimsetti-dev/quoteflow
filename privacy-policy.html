1. Introduction

Welcome to QuoteFlow, a motivational quotes app designed to inspire you every day. This Privacy Policy describes how QuoteFlow ("we," "our," or "the app") handles information when you use our Android application.

QuoteFlow is developed and maintained by Suresh Peddimsetti.

This privacy policy reflects the data handling practices described in the Google Play Data Safety section.

We have designed QuoteFlow with privacy as a core principle. The app does not require you to create an account and does not ask for your name or email address. We use aggregated and non-personal data through analytics and crash reports to improve the app's performance and content for all users. This data is not used to identify individual users. By using the app, you acknowledge the data handling practices described in this policy. This Privacy Policy is publicly accessible and available within the app and on the Google Play Store listing.

2. Information Collection and Use

QuoteFlow is built on a privacy-first foundation. We do not collect personal identifiers like your name, email, or phone number.

To ensure a stable and high-quality experience, we collect aggregated and non-personal data that is not used to identify individual users through third-party services. This data is used solely for analytics and app functionality purposes.

We do not use this data for advertising purposes and we do not sell or share user data with third parties.

The categories of data collected include:

  • Device Information: Operating system version, device model, and technical hardware details.
  • Device Identifiers: An app-instance ID and, where available, the Android Advertising ID (ADID), used by Firebase Analytics to distinguish unique app installations. This identifier is not used for advertising purposes.
  • App Activity: Non-personal usage events, such as screen views and feature interactions (e.g., categories opened).
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance metrics to help us fix bugs and prevent technical errors.

Your saved favorite quotes are stored locally on your device only using Android's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

3. App Permissions & Usage

To provide core app functionality, QuoteFlow requests specific permissions from your device. Each permission is used solely to provide features you explicitly interact with, following Google Play's Limited Use policies.

🌐

Internet Access

Required

Used to fetch quote content and ensure the secure transmission of analytics and crash data. This data is non-personal and is used to improve the app's technical stability and performance.

🔔

Post Notifications

Optional

Used solely to deliver daily motivational reminders (opt-in only). Reminders are scheduled locally on-device at a user-defined frequency and can be disabled anytime in Settings. No notification analytics or open rates are tracked or transmitted.

No other sensitive permissions are requested. QuoteFlow has been built to function with the minimum necessary level of access to your device.

4. Third-Party Services

We use industry-standard services from Google to monitor app performance and improve your experience. These services collect only non-personal technical identifiers as described in Section 2.

QuoteFlow integrates the following services provided by Google LLC:

  • Firebase Analytics: Helps us understand how the app is used so we can improve content and features. All data is aggregated and non-personal, and is not used to identify individual users.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Provides crash reports that help us identify and fix bugs promptly.

Data collected through Firebase services is processed and stored by Google in accordance with their privacy policies. Firebase data may be processed and stored in any country where Google operates data centers. These services may collect technical identifiers used for service-level diagnostics in a non-identifying way. For more information, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Children's Privacy

QuoteFlow is designed for users aged 13 and older. The app does not collect personal identifiers from any user.

Any non-personal technical data collected for analytics and diagnostics is aggregated and used solely to maintain app stability and improve the user experience. This data is not used to identify individual users, nor is it used for profiling or advertising.

6. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

Continued use of QuoteFlow after any changes to this policy constitutes your acceptance of those changes.

7. Data Security

We take the security of your information seriously. Any technical data transmitted to our service providers (Google Firebase) is protected using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL). Your locally saved data is protected by your device's operating system security features.

Since we do not collect personal identifiers such as names or email addresses, we are unable to fulfill individual data deletion requests for analytics data. You may reset your app's analytics identifier by clearing the app's data in your device settings under Apps > QuoteFlow > Clear Data.
